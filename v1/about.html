This site has been created as part of a project for a HND Software Development Course at Dundee and Angus College. It has been made in the first year of the course, so there will be plenty room for improvement. A site about bagpipes was chosen in the hope that this could eventually become a place that has a collection of sheet music and recordings in order to aid pipers with their learning and playing. Pages about the history and the uniforms that are associated with pipe bands have been added for content and in case anyone finds it interesting.
